Both a freelance writer and photo-journalist, has worked and produced content for many Canadian and news outlets, including Radio-Canada, Télé-Québec, Rabble.ca, and Our Times Canada’s Independent Labour Magazine. Along with co-author Laurent Bastien Corbeil, Nicolas was named finalist for the 2013 Canadian University Press award for News Reporting (french). His interests include student activism, language politics and labour issues.
