About Us

{Young}ist is your source for youth-led, fiercely independent political and cultural news, anaylsis, storytelling, and creative intervention by and for Millenials-in-action!

What We Do

{Young}ist was born in response to the ongoing underrepresentation of young people in mainstream media narratives and traditional publishing spaces.

We are dedicated to bringing together and empowering those young people who've been most underserved by corporate news media through delivering fresh, fiercely independent news and views by and for Millenials-in-action.

{Young}ist is a young people-powered platform for citizen journalism, digital storytelling, creative intervention, and more aimed at elevating the stories and struggles of youth across the globe.

Our Mission
  1. Take leadership in building a youth=powered mediascape that is reponsive and accountable to young people who've been underserved by mainstream media outlets
  2. Network and amplify the stories of young people leading transformative projects in their communities
  3. Foster vibrant conversation between young activists, journalists, and culture creatives by delivering a diversity of content - from cultural analyisis and artistic intervention, to political news and movement storytelling
