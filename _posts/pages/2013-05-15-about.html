About Us

{Young}ist is a young people-powered site devoted to storytelling, creative intervention, and news analysis with all staff and contributors under the age of 26.

What We Do

We’re bringing together artists and activists to cross-pollinate and tell our stories as a generation.We publish striking multimedia content and written features, expressive long-form journalism, and innovative art forms, providing a wide range of perspectives on culture and politics—all through the eyes and fingers of youth.

Our Mission
  1. 1. Foster collaboration between artists and activists through sharing our ideas, images, and stories about culture and politics.
  2. 2. Challenge the belief that young artists and activists’ efforts are at odds with one another by putting forward our own accounts of the issues impacting our communities
  3. 3. Develop a youth-powered mediascape by building an online network of young people leading innovative projects to transform the way we produce and consume media
Statement of Principles
  1. 1. We nurture change by providing a dynamic online space that’s run by us and for us
  2. 2. We reject any influence of advertisers or big donors, and make all decisions regarding {young}ist content and design according to principles of participatory self-management
  3. 3. We are accountable to our extended community of artists and organizers, while still maintaining our autonomy.
  4. 4. We strive in both content and conduct to interrogate and dismantle systems of privilege, power, and oppression in society at large; {young}ist strives to highlight and amplify the stories and voices of frontline communities left out of prevailing movement narratives.
  5. 5. We value the privacy of our users, and will allow neither government nor corporations to access user information.
