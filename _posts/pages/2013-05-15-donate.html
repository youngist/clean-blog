{Young}ist exists thanks to money from people who believed in us, people like you, and we wish to continue bringing you content that inspires.

In the past year since {Young}ist launched, we’ve published nearly 300 stories by young people across the globe, and the enthusiastic response has blown all of us away. We’ve grown to upwards of 18,000 readers/month, and our stories have been picked up by everyone from TruthOut and The Nation, to The Daily Beast and Cosmo. Readers can tell we’re different; our contributors are not just writing about movements, they’re helping lead them.

We’ve been bootstrapping it, but we also take pride in paying our contributors for their hard work, sometimes the first paid writing gig they’ve ever had. Just as {Young}ist provides a vital answer to Millennial-bashing in mainstream media narrative, such modest compensation is vital in a mediascape where young writers’ labor is routinely exploited.

But because {Young}ist has grown faster than we could ever have hoped, we need your support now more than ever.

We know that budgets are tight, but if you’ve read, and loved, a {Young}ist story this year, consider chipping in as a thank-you for a job well done, or an investment in expanding this great content’s reach.
